About the System

Our system integrates IoT components such as Arduino, GPS, GSM, accelerometers, and temperature sensors to monitor real-time accident data, ensuring prompt assistance and insightful analytics.

Hardware Components

  • Arduino: Acts as the central controller for processing sensor data.
  • GPS Module: Provides the exact location of the accident in real-time.
  • GSM Module: Sends automated alerts via SMS to emergency services and contacts.
  • Accelerometer: Detects sudden impact or abnormal motion patterns.
  • Temperature Sensor: Monitors temperature fluctuations in case of fire or collision.
  • Heartbeat Sensor: Tracks the victim's pulse to assess their condition.
  • Camera Module: Captures images of the scene for further analysis.
